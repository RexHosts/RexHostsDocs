Creating An Account Last updated: April 4th, 2022

Creating an account is a simple and quick process. This page will walk you through on how to register on RexHosts so you can start building your service!

Create Your Account

To create your account, head over to https://rexhosts.com. You should be in the very main page of RexHosts website - it will look like this:

  • At the very top of the page, you will see a link/button that says register, click that. You will be brought to our sign up page.

  • Fill out all the required fields, then hit the submit button at the bottom of the page. You will then have a registered account with RexHosts!
